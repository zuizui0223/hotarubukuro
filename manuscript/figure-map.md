# Publication figure map

The four composite figures are generated by
`scripts/build_publication_figures.R`. PNG files are embedded in the review
DOCX; vector PDFs are the journal-submission masters. Colours use a
blue–magenta palette with shape, line, or position redundancy where categories
are compared.

## Figure 1 — Two-part digital phenotype

- **Reader question:** How are the extracted display colours distributed
  geographically, and how do they relate to the two response stages?
- **Panels:** national observation map filled with each observation's extracted
  median sRGB value; CIELAB a* distribution and mixture boundary; national
  white-like/pigmented observations; pigmented-only conditional intensity.
- **Sources:** `Data_S1.csv`, `analysis_data_pigmentation_hurdle.csv`,
  `multiscale_hotspot_cells_1km.csv`, and
  `pigmentation_mixture_components.csv`.
- **Claim ceiling:** human-visible colour extracted from uncalibrated sRGB
  photographs; neither anthocyanin concentration nor pollinator colour
  contrast.

## Figure 2 — Environment and spatial structure

- **Reader question:** Which conditional environmental clines were supported,
  and what geographic structure remained?
- **Panels:** environmental posterior coefficients; SPDE range summaries;
  cross-fitted pigmentation probability; cross-fitted pigmented-only
  intensity.
- **Sources:** `pigmentation_hurdle_inla_fixed_effects.csv`,
  `pigmentation_hurdle_inla_hyperparameters.csv`, and the two locked
  national predictive-replication checkpoints.
- **Claim ceiling:** conditional associations and withheld-geography
  predictions, not causal environmental effects or isolated SPDE-only fields.

## Figure 3 — Predicted Bombus information across scales

- **Reader question:** Does the predicted five-species community fingerprint
  add national predictive information or correspond to local flower-colour
  turnover?
- **Panels:** paired held-out AUC on common support; 25-km natural-map
  references for presence and intensity; 10-, 25-, and 50-km sensitivity.
- **Sources:** `predictive_replication_model_fold_performance.csv`,
  `local_pair_predictive_summary.csv`, and
  `local_pair_predictive_null.csv`.
- **Claim ceiling:** predicted habitat-suitability fingerprint, not Bombus
  abundance, visitation, selection pressure, or a causal biotic effect.

## Figure 4 — Local isolates and human context

- **Reader question:** Were locally isolated pigmented cells more frequent than
  expected under the natural model, and did several human-origin proxies
  converge?
- **Panels:** candidate locations; count/fraction replicated-natural-map
  checks; population and DID contrasts; candidate-level early/dark tail tests.
- **Sources:** `local_isolate_candidates.csv`,
  `local_isolate_natural_null.csv`,
  `human_neighbourhood_population_scale_summary.csv`,
  `did_contrast_summary.csv`, and `did_candidate_details.csv`.
- **Claim ceiling:** follow-up priorities and directional human context, not
  horticultural provenance or genetic introgression.
