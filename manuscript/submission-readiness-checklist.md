## Interpretation gates

- CIELAB a* is visible image colour, not anthocyanin concentration or a
  bumblebee receptor contrast;
- ENMeval output is relative habitat suitability, not abundance, richness,
  visitation, or pollination effectiveness;
- the local *Bombus* result is turnover correspondence, not
  pollinator-mediated selection;
- the route-restricted YAMAP frame can attenuate human-context contrasts and
  confound accessibility with exposure; and
- the human-context analyses do not establish horticultural introduction,
  escape, introgression, or genetic pollution.
