# Final locked analysis pipeline

The confirmatory core is the v11 hierarchical phenotype and v16 national environment-plus-SPDE predictive replication.

The planned local mechanism test is the v17 response-blind 25-km pair analysis of flower-colour turnover and predicted Bombus community-fingerprint turnover.

V20 defines local pigmented isolates under the natural maps. V21 population and v22 DID analyses characterize human context after candidate selection and remain exploratory.

Residuals are not primary responses. Individual Bombus species effects, abundance, visitation, horticultural origin, planting, escape, and introgression are not inferred.

`final.Rmd` is intentionally not modified.
